Heartwon Rockfest

2020

HEARTWON ROCKFEST is the largest heavy music festival in Taiwan. Heavy music includes hard rock, punk and metal are represented. Over 100 artists from different countries attend this event.

“Metal is classical music with distortion.” In this design, a classical angel sculpture with rebelion graffiti represents young and wild spirit of this event. Bold typrography with spikes shows the aggresive feeling of heavy music genres performed in this festival.
